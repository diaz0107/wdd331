Determining Selector Support With selector()

If your browser supports the of S clause for the nth-of-type() selector, matching elements will have a dashed, 10 pixel, dashed orange border.

If it does not, the second hexagon (and third path element below) will be blue.

As of July 2021, Safari is the only browser to support this syntax. This may have changed by the time you view this demo.
