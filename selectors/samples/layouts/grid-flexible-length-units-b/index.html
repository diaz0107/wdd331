Mixing Flex Units and Length Units

This example combines flex units and a traditional length as part of its grid-template-columns definition: grid-template-columns: 25rem 2fr 1fr. Notice that the second column is about twice as wide as the third. This proportion remains consistent as long as there is enough leftover space within the container.
