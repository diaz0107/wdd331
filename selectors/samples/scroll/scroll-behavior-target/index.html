Using scroll-behavior and :target

This demo may cause dizziness if you have a vestibular disorder.

This demo may not work as intended if you've disabled animations in your operating system's settings (also known as prefers-reduced-motion).

The scroll-behavior property works whether you're scrolling horizontally or vertically. In some cases, you may wish use the :target pseudo-class to highlight the element to which you've scrolled.
