Enabling or disabling motion in your operating system

Windows 10

  1. Open the Settings menu
  2. Go to Ease of Access
  3. Go to the Display panel
  4. Select or deselect Show animations in Windows

macOS

  1. Open System Preferences
  2. Under the Accessibility panel, choose Display
  3. Select the Reduce Motion setting

Ubuntu Linux

Prerequisite: Have gnome-tweaks installed (sudo apt install gnome-tweaks)

  1. Open gnome-tweaks either from the command line, or by opening Tweaks from your applicatio menu.
  2. In the General (or Appearance) pane, click the Animations toggle to enable or disable animation. When Animations is disabled, perfers-reduced-motion media queries are applied.
