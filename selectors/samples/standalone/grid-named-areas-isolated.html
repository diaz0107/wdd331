Tuna with zucchini noodles

Prep time: 40 minutes. Cook time: 30 minutes. Total: 1 hour, 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh tuna
  • 1 large zucchini, cut into noodle-like spirals
  • 1 14oz can crushed plum tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Throw everything into a warm skillet.

Cook the fish until it's flaky and delicious and the zucchini is no longer crunchy.

This is obviously not a real recipe, but these ingredients probably would make a pretty tasty meal.
