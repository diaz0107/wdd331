Rotating by more than 360 degrees

transform: rotate(900deg); transition: 2s;

This demo may cause dizziness if you have a vestibular disorder.

Rotation transforms that exceeds 360 degrees (1 turn, 400 gradians, or 6.28 radians), are rendered according to their remaindered value. However, if the rotation transform is animated, the animation rotates the specified number of degrees. In this case, the circle will complete 2.5 turns.
